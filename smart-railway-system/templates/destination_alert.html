Travel Tips
Before Journey
  • Set up alert at least 1 hour before departure
  • Verify your contact details are correct
  • Keep your phone charged and accessible
  • Enable notifications on your device
During Journey
  • Keep phone volume on or vibration enabled
  • Check email notifications regularly
  • Inform co-passengers about your destination
  • Prepare luggage when alert is received
