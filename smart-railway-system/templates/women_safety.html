Safety Tips for Women Travelers
Do's
  • Travel in ladies compartment when available
  • Keep emergency contacts saved on your phone
  • Stay in well-lit areas of the station
  • Inform family about your journey details
  • Keep railway security number handy: 1512
Don'ts
  • Don't travel alone late at night if possible
  • Don't accept food/drinks from strangers
  • Don't share personal information unnecessarily
  • Don't ignore uncomfortable situations
  • Don't hesitate to seek help when needed
